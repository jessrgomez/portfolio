Web design with a human touch About Her,

Hola! She is Jessica Gomez, but you can call her Jes. A 27 years old designer and a developer.

Jes is a Front-End Developer with over 6 years of professional experience across web development, systems analysis, data management, and administrative support. She demonstrates strong technical expertise in building scalable, responsive, and performance-optimized web applications using Vue.js, React.js, PHP, and Node.js, complemented by her solid understanding of UI/UX principles and modern front-end frameworks like BootstrapVue, Material UI, and Highcharts Vue. Her work has spanned multiple industries, including aviation, travel, and administration, with hands-on involvement in developing systems. Jes balances her technical skills with strong soft skills, excelling in client communication, documentation, project management, and team mentorship. Aside from development, Jes has administrative and data entry experience, with proven proficiency in MS Office, Google Workspace, and various collaboration platforms such as Jira, GitHub, Bitbucket, and Trello.

Her educational background includes a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, majoring in Web Development, from Holy Angel University, where she was part of a academic awardee, Dean's Lister. She has participated in various technical seminars and earned certifications in Data Privacy Compliance and AWS webinars, showcasing her continuous pursuit of professional development. Jessica is fluent in both English and Filipino, and is recognized for her visual design sense.
